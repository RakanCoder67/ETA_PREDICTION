How it Works

1. Data Collection
Loads standard satellite parameters and matches them with space weather indexes like F10.7 solar flux.
2. Anomaly Check
Determines if the satellite is crossing the Equatorial Thermosphere Anomaly band near the equator.
3. Apply Correction
Calculates position errors and adjusts the predicted coordinates to keep them closer to reality.
