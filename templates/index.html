Starlink Orbit Correction Simulator

Starlink satellites fly in Low Earth Orbit. When they cross the Earth's equator (the Equatorial Thermosphere Anomaly region), atmospheric drag changes unpredictably. Standard physics models (SGP4) drift off course. Our Machine Learning model fixes these errors.

How it Works

1. Data Collection
Loads standard satellite positions and matches them with live space weather indexes.
2. Anomaly Check
Checks if the satellite is crossing the high-drag Equatorial Thermosphere Anomaly region.
3. Apply Correction
Calculates position errors and adjusts predicted coordinates to match reality.
